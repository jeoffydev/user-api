About Us

Kiwi Kids Stories is a non profit organization. The purpose of this app is for those children will learn on how to create their own stories or imagination with parents guidance.

The developer, Jeoffy Hipolito is using this app to check his children's ability and for them to enhance their creativity of doing such thing.

Parents can teach their kids on how to create sentence, paragraph and the basic punctuation.

Kids can:

  • Register and login
  • Create their story
  • Update their story
  • Select font style for each of their story
  • View other kids stories
  • Click Love icon to each story they loved.
  • Create a background colour (soon...)
  • Delete their story (soon)
